ABOUT US



Since Grocery shoppers often get lost in the store aisles while looking for items to purchase, I created a website help people while shopping.


It helps find items throughout the grocery store without having to bother workers who are restocking and busy doing other things.


My invention is important because it will make trips to the grocery store easier and faster.


People who do instacart will make more money because it takes faster to go in and out of the store.
